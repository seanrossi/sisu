"Shine |Y|our Light"

In Finnish, SISU means "hope in the face of adversity." We aim to be a beacon of light as women in STEM undergo #MeToo situations in academic or professional workspaces.

SISU's novel platform includes cases from real women as well as custom solutions and resources provided by diverse field experts.

Empower Now Program

The Empower Now Program is a partnership between SISU and universities or corporations.

Its objective is to build customized cases and workshops, as well as interactive exercises and questionnaires, for clients to integrate within their curriculum or enterprise.
